Achievements

Nationally, Melbourne is among the top-performing universities for competitive research funding, PhD completions and refereed research publications. The University is committed to maintaining excellence in research and development. On this page, you will find a quick access to:

Research report A comprehensive listing of University research activities.
Locate a wide range of research projects by academic subject.

Innovation report Collaborative participation in innovation.
Innovation 2001-2 highlights the University of Melbourne’s extensive interaction with government and industry, our applied research profile and achievements in the transfer of new knowledge and technology.

Performance measurement Extensive documentation of our principal research performance parameters.
Discover how the University of Melbourne benchmarks its performance and the public benefits of its research against the very best universities in Australia and overseas.

Management and training plan Continuously developing research excellence 2003-05.

Miegunyah fellowships Supporting exceptional researchers since 1993.
The Miegunyah Fellowship program encourages scholars of world acclaim to research and share knowledge at the University of Melbourne.
